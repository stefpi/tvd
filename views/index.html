What is twitchdl?

twitchdl is a twitch.tv clip downloader inspired by clipr.xyz. The purpose of twitchdl is to allow anyone to get multiple clip download links at the same time. So assemble your stash, input it above and enjoy!

How to use it?

Step 1: Choose your clips. Do not exceed 30 clips at a time.
Step 2: Enter your clips in the input area above, with a comma (,) after each clip link.
Step 3: Press the download button and see all of your clips on the same page.
Step 4: Click the thumbnail of the clip to download the clip.

If it doesn't work. Wait a minute and try again.
